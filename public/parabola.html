Parabola speed

The second space speed (parabolic speed, release speed, escape speed) is the smallest speed that must be given to an object starting from the surface of a celestial body (for example, a spacecraft), the mass of which is negligible compared to the mass of a celestial body, for (for example, a planet) overcoming the gravitational attraction of this celestial body and leaving the closed orbit around it.
